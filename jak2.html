Synopsis

The story begins immediately following the events of The Precursor Legacy. Jak and friends find this mysterious machine with coordinates, and attempt to travel through a rift gate. Things get hectic in the process, and it backfires. The crew gets split up within the rift gate, and Jak and Daxter get shot out of the sky into a mysterious, industrial-style city. Jak is immediately seized by these strange guards in bright red armor, while Daxter scurries off with the promise of finding Jak again. A whopping two years passes before Daxter finally finds Jak, in the Baron's Prison. It's there that Jak is experimented on with dark eco and given some uncontrollable, dangerous powers. The two break out of the prison to find themselves in Haven City, home to many shady characters. The two begin their quest to find their way home.
