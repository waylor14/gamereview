Synopsis


Jak and Daxter: The Precursor Legacy is the first of an immersive platforming trilogy. The series starts off in a cozy place called Sandover Village; home to our protagonists.

The story follows two friends, named Jak and Daxter. The two have a bad habit of not listening to their wise caretaker, Samos the Sage. Despite constant warning from the old man, Jak and Daxter "borrowed" the local fisherman's boat to venture to Misty Island; a dangerous place crawling with ravenous beasts known as lurkers.

After sneaking onto Misty Island the boys begin snooping around, when they stumble upon two villanous-looking people instructing a crowd of lurkers. After the crowd disperses, the boys continue exploring around and find a giant pool of dark-eco; a magical precursor essence which contains dark power. A lurker brute appears suddenly and lunges towards the boys.

Jak being the brave type he is, picks up a precursor relic and attempts to use it in self defense. Unfortunately, the blast launches him backward into Daxter, who is standing directly in front of the dark-eco pool. A few moments later and something emerges from the pool, but it doesn't look like Daxter...

What emerges from the pool of dark-eco certainly is Daxter, but he's a little bit shorter. Instead of coming back out normally, he returns from the pool in a new form; the form of an Ottsel. An Ottsel is a hybrid between an otter and a weasel, with orange and yellow fur.

The duo immediately starts panicking and promptly return home to Sandover Village. They go to the one person who may know a thing or two about this stuff; Samos. Unfortunately for the boys, not only do they get lectured by the sage, but Samos also admits that he doesn't know the full extent of power that dark-eco has.

Not all hope is lost, however. Samos begins to speak of someone who studies dark-eco and may know a way to return Daxter to his human form. Far, far to the north lives another sage; Gol Acheron, the dark-eco sage. Samos explains how Gol is the only one who would know how to revert the effects of the dark eco on Daxter.

From this moment on, the duo of Jak and Daxter embark on their long journey to far away lands, encountering other sages, monsters, and dangerous terrain. Crossing through jungles, mountains, volcanos, and oceans to reach this dark-eco expert.
